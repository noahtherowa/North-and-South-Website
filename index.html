🎮 About the Game

North and South is a cooperative 2D platformer where two players must work together to overcome impossible odds. Navigate through 3 progressively challenging levels filled with enemies and hazards, then face the Demon King himself in an epic final battle.

📊
Three Challenging Levels

Progress from platforming basics to near-impossible feats

👹
Mystical Enemies

Avoid glowing green monsters roaming the danger zone

🔥
Deadly Hazards

Navigate treacherous lava blocks and environmental traps

👑
Epic Boss Battle

Defeat the Demon King and free Earth from eternal separation

⚙ Core Mechanics

🤝 Proximity Power

Stay close to your partner to unlock special abilities and gain power for the final confrontation.

🎯 Meet at the Equator

Each level requires both souls to reach the glowing golden Equator simultaneously to progress.

🌑 Inverted Gravity

One soul falls upward, one falls downward - cooperation is essential for survival.

🏆 Victory Condition

Reach the final boss and defeat it together. Only unity can overcome darkness.
